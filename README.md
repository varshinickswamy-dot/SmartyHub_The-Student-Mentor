# SmartyHub_The-Student-Mentor
SmartyHub is a smart student productivity platform that integrates a Career Guidance System, AI Chatbot, Resume Generator, and Study Timer/Monitor in one place. It helps students plan careers, generate resumes, manage study time, and get instant assistance through an intelligent chatbot.
1. Project Overview

SmartyHub is a web-based platform designed to assist students in managing their academic and career development efficiently. The system integrates multiple student-focused tools such as a Career Guidance System, AI Chatbot, Resume Generator, and Study Timer/Monitor within a single interface.

The objective of this project is to provide students with a centralized platform that supports career planning, productivity management, and academic assistance.

2. Key Features
1. Student Career Guider

Suggests suitable career paths based on student interests and skills

Provides information about different career opportunities

Helps students make informed decisions about their future

2. AI Chatbot Assistant

Provides instant responses to student queries

Helps with academic and career-related questions

Improves accessibility to guidance and information

3. Resume Generator

Allows students to create professional resumes easily

Structured resume templates

Downloadable resume format

4. Study Timer / Student Monitor

Helps students track their study sessions

Improves productivity using timed study intervals

Encourages focused learning
